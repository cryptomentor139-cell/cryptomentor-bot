Capital Protection

Risk management isn't optional. It's the edge.

One oversized loss can erase many small wins. Every layer of the system is designed to keep you in the game long enough to compound.

  • Risk defined as a fixed percentage of equity per trade — never a dollar amount
  • Stop loss placement comes before position size calculation
  • Maximum concurrent exposure is capped across all open positions
  • Correlated pairs are monitored to avoid stacked directional risk
  • Leverage is used as a tool, not a goal — effective exposure stays bounded
  • Drawdown thresholds trigger automatic reduction in activity
